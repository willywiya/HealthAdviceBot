🌬️ Breathing Exercises

We recommend the following simple breathing technique to help with breathing difficulty:

  1. 🪑 Sit upright and relax your shoulders.
  2. 👃 Inhale deeply through your nose for 4 seconds.
  3. ⏸️ Hold your breath for 7 seconds.
  4. 💨 Exhale slowly through your mouth for 8 seconds.
  5. 🔁 Repeat this cycle 3–4 times.

💡 This is called the 4-7-8 method. It's great for calming anxiety and improving airflow.
